Content Check

Problem is Clear You can explain what marine pollution problem you chose in 1–2 sentences.
Evidence is Strong You have 2–3 real data points or observations from Lessons 3–4.

Sources & Citations

At Least 2–3 Reliable Sources (2–3) Government, university, NGO, or verified news — NOT just Wikipedia or Google.
Sources are Cited Your slides show: [Author/Organization] + [Date] + [Website/Report]
Sources are Diverse You don't rely on just 1 organization — mix government, research, and NGO sources.

Presentation Skills

Slides are Clear Big fonts, not too much text, includes visuals (charts, photos).
You Can Explain Practise your 2–3 min pitch — know your facts!
Team is Ready Each person knows their part. Who speaks first/second/third?

Important Reminders!

NO made-up data! Only use REAL observations or published sources.

AI can help you ask better questions, but YOU must verify all facts.

Be ready to answer difficult questions — think about opposite views!

Show respect for all stakeholder perspectives, even if you disagree.
