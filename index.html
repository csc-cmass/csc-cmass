Lesson 8: Stakeholder Meeting & Final Presentation

+  |  60 minutes  |  Conclusion

Learning Goals

  • I can present a solution using evidence and reliable sources.
  • I can respond to questions as a stakeholder and revise my ideas.

Success Criteria

Slides include: Problem → Evidence → Solution → Stakeholders → Sources
Role-play shows: Different viewpoints and respectful discussion

Key Terms

stakeholder (持分者) citation (引用) proposal (建議書) fairness (公平)
